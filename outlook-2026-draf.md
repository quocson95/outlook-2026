### The Core Focus: From Hype to Utility

The overarching theme for 2026 is the **maturation of the industry**. Messari predicts that capital will concentrate on assets with credible monetary properties and products that users rely on daily, rather than "long-term promises."

Trọng tâm chính: Từ sự cường điệu đến tính hữu dụng

Chủ đề bao quát cho năm 2026 là sự trưởng thành của ngành công nghiệp. Messari dự đoán rằng vốn sẽ tập trung vào các tài sản có đặc tính tiền tệ đáng tin cậy và các sản phẩm mà người dùng sử dụng hàng ngày, thay vì "những lời hứa dài hạn".

### . Seven Core Sectors for 2026

The report structures its outlook around seven key pillars:

- **Cryptomoney:** A heavy focus on stablecoins, institutional adoption, and "yield-driven DeFi banks" as the primary ways people will interact with crypto as money.

- **DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure):** Highlighted as a "high-upside frontier." Messari sees a major "dislocation" where the physical infrastructure fundamentals are finally catching up to the narrative.

- **Crypto x AI:** Predicted to reach a **$30 trillion market by 2030**. Specific focus areas include "agentic commerce" (AI agents transacting on-chain), decentralized model training, and robotics.

- **The Multichain World:** Interoperability is no longer a "feature" but a base assumption. The report notes the "L1/L2 wars" are evolving, with Ethereum reclaiming ground through L2s like **Base** and **Arbitrum**, while **Solana** pushes to become the "on-chain NASDAQ."

- **TradFi x Crypto:** The convergence of traditional finance and crypto through equity perpetuals (perps), tokenized Treasuries (RWAs), and maturing regulation.

- **DeFi:** A shift toward sustainable models and "decentralized internet finance" that provides real utility over high-inflation yield farming.

- **Consumer Apps:** Reaching a "tipping point" where crypto-powered products achieve mainstream adoption without the user necessarily needing to know they are using blockchain.
